## Why Spatius?


Traditional digital avatar solutions force painful trade-offs between cost, visual quality, and real-time performance. Spatius solves this trilemma:


| Metric | Traditional Cloud Rendering | Spatius On-Device |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Bandwidth** | 1-2 MB/s (Video Stream) | 10-20 KB/s (Motion Data) |
| **Cost** | High GPU rental costs | Ultra-low edge compute |
| **Latency** | > 3s | < 1.5s |
| **Compatibility** | High-speed network dependent | 99% of Android/iOS/Web devices |


## Use Cases


- **Online Education** - Immersive 1-on-1 tutoring with precise lip-sync for language learning and K12
- **Recruitment & Training** - 7x24h AI interviewers and role play training simulations
- **Customer Service** - Brand ambassadors with visual eye contact that builds emotional connection
- **Social & Entertainment Apps** - Lifelike AI companions and virtual hosts for social platforms and interactive entertainment
- **AI Hardware with Screens** - Bring expressive avatars to smart displays, robots, and other screen-equipped AI devices
